SAMANAR HILLS

Descripition

Samanar hill is Located in Keelakuyilkudi village which is around 12 km away from Madurai, Samanar hills are beautiful blend of nature with history. It also had 12 stone beds, used by the monks to take rest. The hill has been declared as a protected monument by the Archaeological Survey of India. It takes nearly 2 hours to visit the hill.
